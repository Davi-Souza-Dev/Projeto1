If you are interested in participating in TechConnect, the registration process is simple and free! Just fill out the registration form on our main page and wait for confirmation by email. Be sure to provide accurate information to secure your spot at this exclusive technology and innovation event.

To ensure a smooth experience for all attendees, TechConnect will offer parking at the event location. Our team will be available to guide you through available parking options and provide additional information if necessary. Be ready to arrive early and make the most of the event

We understand that plans can change and unforeseen events can arise. If you need to cancel your subscription to TechConnect, please contact us as soon as possible. Although registration is free, we appreciate your cooperation in helping us ensure that all places are used to the best of their ability. We are here to help you with any requests or questions regarding unsubscriptions.
